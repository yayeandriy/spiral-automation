Landing
- Zones: a zone is major part of landing, there could be up to 5 zones on the landing. 
The best desc of zone is that a zone could be a separate web page without (or almost without) 
lost of sence.
— Sections: a section is building brick of a zone. A section has a complete story inside.
— Blocks: a block is building part of a section. One block is a one message. 
It could be any kind of content: text, image, video, gif, etc.

There is a source with a list of links to landings. 
Go through each of them and as a result create json file with detailed desc of 
generalizes types of zones, sections and blocks.

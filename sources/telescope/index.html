Automated Tower Equipment Inspection for Accurate Billing and Inventory Control

Tower companies hosting multiple tenants often struggle with incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate equipment records. Tenants install or modify gear—sometimes without disclosure—and billing based on surface area or number of units quickly becomes unreliable.

Manual audits are resource-intensive. Drone inspections are costly, time-consuming, and require specialized post-processing. The result? Missed revenue, operational inefficiency, and compliance risk.

Telescopeturns ordinary ground-level photos into billing-ready, ERP-synced tower inventories using advanced AI and computer vision.

No drones, no delays—just accurate insights from your field teams' smartphones or cameras.

Capture: Take standard 4K photos from the ground.

Upload: Use the web portal or mobile app.
